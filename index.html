Tips & Tricks

Beginner Tips

Focus on movement speed early on to avoid getting cornered. Always keep moving and try to group enemies together for maximum damage output.

Advanced Strategy

Build synergistic abilities that complement each other. For example, pair area damage skills with slowing effects to control large groups of monsters.
