About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game where you battle endless hordes of monsters. Choose from various characters, each with unique starting weapons and abilities.

The game features:

  • Multiple Characters: Each with unique starting weapons.
  • Weapon Evolution: Combine weapons with passive items.
  • Level Up System: Gain XP and choose new abilities.

Tips & Tricks

Beginner Tips

Focus on movement speed early on to avoid getting cornered. Always keep moving and try to group enemies together for maximum damage output.

For most games in our collection, mastering the basic controls and understanding core mechanics will give you a solid foundation to build upon.

Advanced Strategy

Build synergistic abilities that complement each other. For example, pair area damage skills with slowing effects to control large groups of monsters.

Each game has unique strategies to master. Experiment with different approaches and find what works best for your playstyle.
